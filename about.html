What About Gender Disparities in Healthcare?

by Joshua Essenmacher

In many aspects of healthcare, women are marginalized and discriminated due to biases and lack of inclusivity.

Women spend more but receive less quality care. Women are underrepresented in the health workforce. Across the world, women are less likely to receive health services.

We must strive for gender equality in healthcare; meaning men and women are on equal footing for their rights to be healthy. We must also strive for gender equity, meaning a fair distribution of resources and benefits between men and women.
